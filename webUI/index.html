The goal of anticounterfeiting with blockchain technology aims to provide a safe and impenetrable network for tracking and certifying the legitimacy of products across every step of their supply chain. A transparent and reliable system that enables customers and businesses to confirm the legitimacy of products, prevent counterfeiting, and guarantee that the things they buy are authentic can be made possible by employing blockchain, an immutable distributed ledger technology.
